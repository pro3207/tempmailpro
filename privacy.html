TempMailPro — Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

1. Introduction

TempMailPro provides temporary email addresses for short-term use. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting personal information.

2. Data We Collect

  • Automatically created inboxes: When you generate a temporary email, minimal account data (address, password, token) is used to fetch messages. This may be processed by third-party APIs (e.g., mail.tm).
  • No long-term storage: We do not store your emails permanently on our servers. If you deploy our site to your own server, configure retention as needed.
  • Analytics: We may use Google Analytics or similar for aggregated site metrics. These tools do not collect personally identifying data by us.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the minimal data required to create and display temporary inboxes. We do not share your email contents with third parties except the mail provider required to deliver messages.

4. Ads and Third Parties

We may display ads (Google AdSense). Ad providers may collect certain data (e.g., via cookies) to serve personalized ads. Consult the ad provider's policy for details.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data. However, temporary email services are inherently less secure for sensitive information — do not use them for banking or critical accounts.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us via Contact.
